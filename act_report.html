Conclusion¶

After the whole analysis of the twitter_master dataset, three insights were made;

  • it was discovered that the relationship between the favorite_count and the retweet_count was positive, it means that the number of those almost the number of those that liked the tweet also retweeted it.
  • The golden retriever is the most popular dog amidst the whole breed. it has an average value of 0.019518
  • it was also discovered that the dumber dog_breed is the most rated dog.
